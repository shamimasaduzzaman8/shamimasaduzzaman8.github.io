Research

Research Areas, Projects and Scientific Contributions

Research Philosophy

My research focuses on integrating Artificial Intelligence, Biomedical Engineering, Embedded Systems, Renewable Energy, Internet of Things (IoT), and Optical Fiber Sensors to develop intelligent and sustainable engineering solutions. I aim to bridge theoretical research with practical applications that address healthcare, energy, communication, and industrial challenges.

Research Interests

My research focuses on developing intelligent engineering solutions through interdisciplinary research in artificial intelligence, biomedical engineering, communication systems, and sustainable technologies.

Artificial Intelligence

Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition and AI-based Engineering Applications.
