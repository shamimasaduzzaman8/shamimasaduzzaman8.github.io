Teaching

Empowering future engineers through innovative teaching, hands-on laboratory experiences, and research-driven education.

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy centers on creating an engaging learning environment that combines strong theoretical foundations with practical engineering applications. I encourage students to develop analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and research-oriented mindsets through active participation, laboratory experiments, simulation-based learning, and project-based activities.

As an educator, I believe that engineering education should inspire innovation, ethical responsibility, teamwork, and lifelong learning, preparing students to address real-world technological challenges.
