About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the European University of Bangladesh. My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Biomedical Engineering, Medical Image Analysis, Internet of Things (IoT), Renewable Energy Systems, Embedded Systems, and Optical Fiber Sensors. I am passionate about advancing interdisciplinary research that addresses practical engineering challenges.
