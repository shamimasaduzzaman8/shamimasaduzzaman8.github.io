About Me

Assistant Professor • Department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering

Hello!

I am Md. Asaduzzaman Sarker, Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the European University of Bangladesh.

My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Biomedical Engineering, Medical Image Analysis, Internet of Things (IoT), Renewable Energy Systems, Embedded Systems, and Optical Fiber Sensors.

I am passionate about research, teaching, and developing intelligent engineering solutions that have practical societal impact.
